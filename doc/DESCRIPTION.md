Animarr is a smart Torznab proxy specifically designed for Anime. It sits between your favorite indexers (via Prowlarr) and your download automation tools (Radarr, Sonarr).

Its main purpose is to resolve the mismatch between scene naming and anime metadata providers like AniList, MAL, and TMDB. It automatically maps different IDs and cleans up titles to ensure your automation tools find exactly what you're looking for, in the right quality and language.
